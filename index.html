New Magnifying Glass

The original ColorSnapper magnifying glass – introduced in 2010 and adopted by every modern color picker app since then – just got even better!

It works with multiple displays, different resolutions and with different pixel density. You can switch between Spaces and Applications having the magnifying glass on the screen.

  • Introducing Hi-Precision Mode

    Allows to choose every physical pixel on a retina screen, but also works great on displays with normal pixel density by increasing mouse sensitivity and magnification level.

  • Control with gestures & keyboard

    Zoom in & out, change diameter, switch through formats or move pixel by pixel using gestures and keyboard shourtcuts.

Organize, adjust & export colors with style

ColorSnapper 2 is designed for OS X Yosemite and supports both light and dark themes.

  • Favorites & History

    Mark colors you regularly pick as favorites or go back in time to hundreds of colors.

  • Modify colors

    The new user interface seamlessly integrates with Apple’s Color Panel so you can adjust colors before you export them.

  • Quick formats filter

    Start typing to quickly search through more than 30 export formats.

Match your coding style

ColorSnapper 2 allows you to match the color export format to your coding style. Along with all new suported formats, including: Generic, CSS, NSColor, UIColor, Swift, Android, Java, .NET, CGColor and OpenGL this results in even faster developer workflow.

Accurate color values

The new color picker automatically converts colors picked from native OS X applications, including Safari and Xcode from your monitor’s color profile to sRGB. This means, you’ll get the same 1 RGB values as those used in your code.2

1 There may be small conversion differences when picking colors from monitors with a smaller color space than sRGB.

2 Non-native applications and applications that are not using the Cocoa framework are not affected by conversion issues.

And even more…

  • Photoshop & Illustrator Integration

    Control foreground and background colors directly from ColorSnapper in your favorite designer tools. No settings: it just works!

  • Capture Screenshots

    The new magnifying glass can be used as a review tool. Press P to copy the zoomed image into clipboard.

  • ColorSnapper API Soon

    Having command-line access to the magnifying glass allows developers to write plugins for popular code editors or integrate with graphic software.
