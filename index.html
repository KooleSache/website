New Magnifing Glass

The original ColorSnapper magnifiying glass, introduced in 2010 and adopted by every modern color picker app since then, just got reinvented!

  • Hi-Precision Mode

    Allows choosing every retina pixel on HiDPI screens, but also works great on non-retina displays by slowing down the movement of the mouse and increasing a magnification level.

  • Control using gestures & keyboard

    Zoom, change diameter, switch through formats or move pixel by pixel. You have total and granular control.

Organize, adjust & export colors with style

ColorSnapper 2 designed for OS X Yosemite and support both light and dark themes.

  • Favorites & History

    Add colors you might use ofter to the favorites or go back in time up to one hundred of colors you have already picked.

  • Quick formats filter

    Start typing first letters to quickly search through the more than 30 clipboard formats.

  • Modify colors

    ColorSnapper2 seamlessly integrates with the Apple’s Color Panel so you can adjust colors before you export them.
